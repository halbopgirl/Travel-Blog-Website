Hi, I'm Jayde! I'm a 27-year-old jack of all trades with diverse interests. I am a traveler, musician, artist, athelete, writer, teacher, computer programmer, and Buddhist. My love for travel began with a bicycle trip across the country with my father in my early teens. I then started my own business and traveled the country selling music and clothing, first from a van, and then from an RV. After that didn't work out, I took a detour to Vegas to be a career bartender, but my desire to travel never subsided. Even the flight attendant job which followed did not give me the level of freedom that I craved. Now, I am working remotely, freelancing, and studying computer science in hopes of becoming a full time traveler. Follow my journey, struggles, heartaches, and successes as I learn, love, and see the world.
